VPN Installation Guide for macOS

Important

Referencing the content in this webpage:

Bulusu, Kartik V. (2026, January). MAE 6291: Internet-of-Things for Engineers and Scientists. [Course lecture notes, codes and presentations]. Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, The George Washington University.

Introduction

This guide will take you through the step-by-step process of installing the GWU Virtual Private Network (VPN), on your MacOS system.

Prerequisites

Before you start, make sure you have the following:

  1. A macOS computer.
  2. An active internet connection.
  3. Adequate disk space for the installation.

Installation Steps

1. Visit GWIT’s Software downloads site. Note: You may have to login using GWU NetID to access the site.

Go to https://my.gwu.edu/mod/downloads/ to access GWIT’s Software downloads site.

2. Select the macOS Download Option

Download the Palo Alto Global Protect VPN. Choose the installer for “macOS” option and click to initiate the download.

3. Execute the Installer

Locate the downloaded file, typically in your “Downloads” folder. Double-click the installer executable (“.pkg”) file to run it.

4. Confirm Installation

Follow the Installer Wizard. Follow the on-screen instructions to begin the installation. Select the “GlobalProtect” on the custom install, click “Install”, and enter password.

5. Complete the Installation

Click “Close” to complete installation and keep installer.

6. Initiate Installation

In the top right Taskbar Notification Area, select GlobalProtect. In the GlobalProtect Client menu, click “Get Started” and enter the portal address - “gwvpn.gwu.edu”

7. Connect to GWU VPN

Click Connect and follow the prompts to sign-in using your UserID credentials for single sign-on (SSO) with two-step authentication.

8. Connection Confirmation

After successful sign-in, the GlobalProtect Client will show a successful connection.

Conclusion

Congratulations! GWU VPN is now successfully installed on your macOS system.
